Welcome to the infoEnergy Nexus

We are a working group at the University of California, Berkeley that focuses on the transformative role of information technology on modern energy systems. The group shares knowledge and builds tools together, and is mostly made up of students who are working on our 'own' projects. 

We are be sharing code :)

The general repository is seeded with a 
file that provides easy interface with 
the CAISO OASIS portal for downloading 
CA electricity market data.
